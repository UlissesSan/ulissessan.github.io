About

  • Hi, nice to meet you!

    Let's talk a bit about me. I'm a Brazilian guy interested in build mobile application, because the idea of having a solver problem right on your hand is so interesting.

  • 2012

    Federal Institute of Piauí

    In 2012 after high school I didn't know what to do, although I liked all technological stuff. I pass through a test to ingress in law program (the one that my family wanted) and other to software development program. Lucky I had a my friend that told me not to go to law school, so I did the right choice and went to IFPI.

  • 2013-2014

    Sertão Games

    Almost no one was taking game development seriously in my city, then a studio started investing one it. Fortunately they called me to work with them as game designer, that was the best team and I learned a lot working with them.

  • 2014-2015

    University Exchange

    Then I got the opportunity to do an exchange in Canada. It wasn't easy to leave home, but it was the most amazing experience in my life. Live in a different country, learning new things everyday, new cultures, new language, meeting new people. No words to describe.

  • 2016

    Back in Brazil

    I'm back in Brazil. I worked at InfoG2, a good company with an open mind. Lucky me, another good team learning and doing my first iOS app. Now I'm working on personal projects and finishing my graduation, but always opened to new experiences.
